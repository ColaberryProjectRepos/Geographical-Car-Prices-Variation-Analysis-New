Project Overview 1

Research indicates that geographic location can influence car prices by up to 15%. This project explores the intricate relationship between geography and pricing by employing advanced geospatial analysis and RidgeCV regression model. Through this approach, we identified key factors driving regional price variations. The insights gained provide valuable data to car dealerships and pricing strategists, enabling them to refine their pricing strategies in line with regional market trends.
